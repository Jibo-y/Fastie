Fastie

Your Virtual Transportation Wallet!

Our mission is to make using public transportation faster and easier to use for commuters. The E-metrocard will allow you to pay as you go. This new system offers the commuter the option of a digital metro-card where you can use your credit/debit card, or paypal. Unlike, a regular Metrocard, your account automatically refills. This app will give realtime information on any delays along with different routes. Furthermore, E-metrocard app allow customers to create personalized transit account where they can check their account balance and add any amount at any time. This digital wallet system will help commuters get to their destination faster.
